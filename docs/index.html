故事设定

Story Design

AI 辅助构思世界观、剧情主线、视觉基调。产出 Story 资产，定义整个项目的创作方向。

AI-assisted world-building, plot, and visual tone. Outputs a Story asset that defines the entire project's creative direction.

👤

角色设计

Character Design

自动生成角色定妆 Prompt，保证后续所有镜头中角色形象的一致性。主角支持三视图。

Auto-generates portrait prompts for consistent character appearance across all shots. Lead characters support turnaround sheets.

🎬

场景规划

Scene Planning

将故事拆解为关键场景，每个场景规划镜头序列、时长预估、转场方式。纯规划，不产 Prompt。

Breaks the story into key scenes with shot sequences, timing, and transitions. Planning only — no prompts yet.

🎥

镜头制作

Shot Production

逐镜头生成完整的生图和视频 Prompt。支持首帧/首尾帧模式。Prompt 自动融合角色+场景+视觉基调。

Generates complete image & video prompts per shot. Supports single/dual keyframe modes. Auto-merges character + scene + visual tone.
